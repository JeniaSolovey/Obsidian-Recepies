## Ingredients
- Pasta
- Cherry tomatoes
- Onion
- Garlic
- Parmesan
- Parsley

## Process
1. Start cooking pasta
2. Fry onion
3. Add garlic and salt
4. Add whole tomatoes
5. Cover
6. When tomatoes skin start to split mash tomatoes into sauce
7. Reduce a bit
8. Add pasta
9. Add parmesan and parsley
