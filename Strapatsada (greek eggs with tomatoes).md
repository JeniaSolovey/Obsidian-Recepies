## Ingredients
- Eggs
- Tomatoes
- Garlic
- Feta cheese
- Green onion (optional)
- Fresh bread
## Process
1. Grade tomatoes (or cut on very tiny cubes)
2. Fry garlic
3. Add the tomatoes
4. Add mixed eggs
5. Mix all the time
6. Add feta
7. Serve with fresh bread
